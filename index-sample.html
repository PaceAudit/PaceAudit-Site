How It Works

Live in under 5 minutes

Connect your CRM, upload your handbook, and your entire sales process is enforced automatically — no manual configuration needed.

01
🔗

Connect your CRM

Connect with one click. Deals, contacts, and pipeline sync automatically — no keys to copy, no CSV uploads, no manual setup.

02
📄

Upload your SOP handbook

Drop in any PDF. AI reads it and extracts your email cadence, call cadence, and follow-up windows into fully editable rules.

Features

Everything your CRM was never built to do

🤖
AI SOP Interpretation

Upload any PDF handbook. AI reads your entire SOP and extracts email cadences, call windows, and follow-up rules — no manual setup required.

⚡
Real-Time CRM Sync

Connects directly to your CRM. Activity syncs on a schedule or in real time depending on plan — including live updates so the moment a deadline slips, violations fire before the deal goes cold.

📣
9-Level Escalating Nudges

From a warm friendly reminder to a formal escalation — 9 distinct tone levels. Each notification type gets its own tone and fully customizable message template.

💰
Revenue Recovery Tracking

Tracks overdue deals that close after a PaceAudit nudge, and how many deals were lost when cadence wasn’t followed. See monthly how much revenue your team recovered and where follow-up slipped.

📊
Compliance Dashboard & Leaderboard

Live compliance scores per rep. Violation history. Revenue at risk per open deal. Weekly executive summaries delivered automatically every Monday at 8am.

🔔
Multi-Channel Alerts

Reach every rep on the channel they actually check. Slack, Microsoft Teams, SMS, and email — with per-rep consent controls and per-trigger channel selection.
